TedX Talk

This is my TEDx Talk which I spoke about at my school event. My school had a Tedx club which was optional for students to participate in. I participated in this club because I wanted to tell people what I believe and share my talk to the world so I joined this club. At first, I felt like leaving as everything got boring but then my teachers encouraged me and motivated me to continue and so for the next three months I worked very hard in writing, improvising, and speaking my talk. When I recording my practice version my teachers (who gave me suggestions for my talk) said that I had improved so much from my original speech to my practice recording. They were amazed! Hearing all that feedback influenced me to even further improvise my talk and after another week of hard work, my talk had slides, music, and even special effects. I had the best version of my talk ready and I submitted it. I was extremely proud of the work I had done. 2 days after publishing my talk, I got an email from my teachers saying that my talk had been uploaded to the original TedED website!
